Return & Refund Policy

Effective Date: March 13, 2025

1. Return Eligibility

We accept returns if:

  • The item is **damaged, defective, or incorrect**.
  • The return is initiated within **7 days of delivery**.
  • The product is unused, in **original packaging**, with tags and accessories.

Note: Customized, clearance, or made-to-order furniture cannot be returned.

2. Return Process

  1. Contact us at support@npkinterior.com or call +91-92939495.
  2. Our team will verify your request and provide further instructions.
  3. Once approved, we will arrange a pickup or guide you to ship it back.
  4. After the return is received and inspected, we will process the refund/exchange.

3. Refund Policy

  • Refunds are processed to the **original payment method** within **5-7 business days**.
  • For **Cash on Delivery (COD)** orders, refunds are given via **bank transfer or store credit**.
  • Shipping charges are **non-refundable**, unless the return is due to our error.

4. Exchange Policy

We offer exchanges for the same item if in stock. Otherwise, a refund will be processed.

To exchange for a different product, return the original item and place a new order.

5. Non-Returnable Items

  • Customized, personalized, or made-to-order furniture.
  • Used, damaged, or altered products (unless received that way).
  • Items missing original packaging or proof of purchase.

6. Contact Us

For any queries, email us at support@npkinterior.com or call **+91-92939495**.
